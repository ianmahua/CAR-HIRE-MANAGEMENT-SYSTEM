Section 4: Hirer Responsibilities

The Hirer agrees to:

  • Return the vehicle in the same condition as received, fair wear and tear excepted.
  • Pay for all fuel, oil, and other consumables used during the hire period.
  • Comply with all traffic laws, regulations, and local road rules.
  • Ensure that only authorised and licensed drivers operate the vehicle.
  • Use the vehicle solely for lawful purposes and in a careful and responsible manner.
  • Immediately report any accident, damage, theft, or mechanical issues to the Company.

Section 5: Insurance & Liability

The vehicle is covered by a comprehensive insurance policy, subject to the terms and conditions of the insurer. However, the Hirer acknowledges and agrees that they shall be liable for:

  • Any damage to the vehicle arising from negligence, misuse, or breach of this Agreement.
  • Theft or loss of the vehicle where the keys are left in the vehicle or it is left unsecured.
  • Any insurance excess or deductibles specified in the insurance policy.
  • All costs, penalties, or fines not covered by the insurance policy.

Important: In the event of an accident, the Hirer must obtain a police abstract and promptly provide all necessary information and assistance required by the Company and the insurer.

Section 6: Prohibited Use

The vehicle shall not be used for any of the following purposes:

  • Participation in any race, rally, competition, or use on non-designated roads or off-road terrain.
  • Driving under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any other intoxicating substance.
  • Carrying passengers or goods for hire or reward, unless expressly authorised in writing.
  • Sub-letting, lending, or permitting any unauthorised person to drive the vehicle.
  • Transporting illegal goods or engaging in any unlawful activities.
  • Driving the vehicle outside the borders of Kenya without prior written approval from the Company.
